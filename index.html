FAQ

Questions we get asked a lot.

How accurate is the transcription? +
MemoryMeet uses OpenAI's Whisper model — the same technology powering transcription for leading research institutions. For clear English audio, expect under 10% word error rate. You can also correct any errors before saving.
What file formats are supported? +
We accept MP3, MP4, M4A, WAV, and WebM files up to 500 MB per upload. This covers Zoom exports, Google Meet recordings, Loom videos, voice memos, and most other meeting formats.
How is my meeting data stored and secured? +
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Recording files are deleted after processing unless you opt to retain them. We never use your meeting content to train AI models without explicit consent.
Can I invite my whole team? +
Yes. Free workspaces include 1 member. Pro plans include up to 10 members. Team plans include up to 20. All members share the workspace meeting memory — so everyone can query past decisions.
Does it integrate with Zoom, Slack, or Notion? +
Not yet — but they're coming. For the MVP, you upload recordings manually. Zoom and Google Meet bots, plus Slack and Notion exports, are planned for the first major update after launch.
What languages are supported? +
English is fully supported at launch. Whisper supports 99 languages, and we plan to enable multilingual support in a future update. Join the waitlist and let us know your language — it helps us prioritise.
When does MemoryMeet launch? +
We're targeting a Q3 2026 beta launch. Waitlist members will get early access and founding member pricing locked in at launch. Join now to secure your spot.
How does Memory Chat actually work? +
Every meeting is broken into chunks and converted into semantic vectors stored in a vector database. When you ask a question, we find the most relevant chunks, then use GPT-4o to synthesise a sourced answer — only from your meetings, never fabricated.
